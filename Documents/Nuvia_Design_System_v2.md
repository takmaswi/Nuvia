## GLASSMORPHISM EFFECT (Modern, Contemporary)

### **What is Glassmorphism?**
Semi-transparent, frosted-glass effect with blur backdrop filter. Creates depth, elegance, and contemporary feel.

### **Why It's Perfect for Nuvia + White Background**

âœ… **Visual Impact**: Frosted glass POPS on white backgrounds
âœ… **Contemporary**: 2025 design trend, not dated
âœ… **Premium Feel**: Luxury aesthetic without being heavy
âœ… **Photography Integration**: Cards overlay images beautifully
âœ… **Functional**: Creates depth hierarchy
âœ… **Modern React**: Native to Framer, Webflow, modern frameworks

### **CSS Implementation**
